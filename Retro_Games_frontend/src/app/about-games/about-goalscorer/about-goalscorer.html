How to play

To start playing, press the “Space” key.
If you are in the middle of a game and don't like what you see, press the green button — labeled “Restart”.
If you have just lost the game and want to try again, press “Play Again” (yes, it's the same green button).
To return to the first page of the game, press the red “Back” button.
If you want to take a break — relax, drink a coffee, and then continue playing to beat your high score — press the orange “Pause” button to stop, and “Resume” to continue.
If you are tired of the game, simply press the blue “Logout” button.

Playing the game is very simple:
You have to move the paddle left or right (using the Left and Right keys) so that the ball does not collide with the goalkeeper or the walls and goes between the goalposts.

Rules

The rules are also simple:
You have to score goals to earn points. It's necessary to score a lot of goals to be among the top five on the leaderboard.
You lose when the ball goes down besides the paddle.
When you score goals, the level increases and both the ball and the goalkeeper move faster.

History of the game

Goal Scorer is a game that could be found on 90s consoles among other brick games, such as Tetris and Snake.
